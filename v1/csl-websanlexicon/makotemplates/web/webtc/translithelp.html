Cologne Sanskrit-Lexicon Transliteration Help

Words must be entered in the transliteration scheme chosen by the user. There are three supported transliterations:

  • HK = Harvard-Kyoto (or Kyoto-Harvard)
  • SLP1 = Sanskrit Library Phonetic
  • ITRANS = Indian languages TRANSliteration
